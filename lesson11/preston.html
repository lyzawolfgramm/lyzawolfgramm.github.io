COVID-19 VACCINE AVAILABLE

For Ages 50 Years+ and ages 16 Years+ with certain underlying medical conditions.

Who is eligible for the Vaccine?

Vaccines will be available to those who are 50 Years+ and ages 16 Years+ with certain underlying medical conditions. You can find more information regarding the following medical conditions eligible for the COVID-19 vaccine at the www.prestoncountyhealthdepartment.com/

When and how do I get the Vaccine?

Vaccines will be available Saturday March 26th - April 9th, 2021 Monday - Friday 9am - 6pm. You must schedule an appointment for your Covid-19 Vaccine ahead of time to ensure there is enough vaccines for each person during their alloted time. Please do not just show up without an appointment everyone must have an appointment. You can schedule your appointment one of two ways:
1 - Visit www.prestoncountyhealthdepartment.com click "schedule an appointment" and fill in the information needed. You will then receive a confirmation and code to confirm your appointment was made.
2 - Or you can call (833)734-0965 and one of our agents can help you schedule an appointment.

Do you need Insurance to get a Vaccine?

The vaccines are FREE and you do not need insurance to receive a vaccine shot. The only requirements needed are to register and to bring a photo ID.
