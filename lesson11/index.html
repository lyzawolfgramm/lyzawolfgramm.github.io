Eastern Idaho State Fair returning this summer July 2021!

Covid-19 had taken all of us by surprise. Communities and Events and have been heavily effected an our small towns of Preston, Soda springs and Fish Haven have felt deserted since quarantine. As Eastern Idaho is slowly opens back up we are feeling blessed for the opportunities to gather again soon. Although the State Fair is opening back up this summer attendance will be limited. Please watch the website for announcements of when tickets will be going on sale. Along with the dates and times of the event.

Tickets can only be purchased online and there will not be anyway to purchase a ticket at the event. Every person who purchases a ticket online will be emailed a qr code. Please have that qr code ready to to be scanned before entering the event. You will not be required a mask at this event but if you feel safe wearing one please do so. This State Fair will be one like never before. We will still have the old town feel but you will feel a difference at this event. A good difference! Many members on our committee have spent hours planning this event and have taken everyones safety into consideration and planned accordingly. We look forward to everyone joining us at the fair and can't wait to bring Eastern Idaho back to life!!!
