JOIN US!

The Provo Chamber of Commerce is a non-profit, member supported private organization that promotes economic growth and prosperity in Provo City, Utah. Our focus is to strengthen and support local businesses, influence, lead and advocate for public policies, communities and local businesses and be a resource to the community.

MEMBERSHIP

Different Levels

Non-Profit: Subscription to monthly newsletter, directory listing.

Bronze: Non-Profit benefits in addition to advertisement package, Social media marketing and business trainings.

Silver: Non-Profit & Bronze benefits in addition to business mentor, website services, zoom meetings access and luncheons.

Gold: all benefits of previous packages plus all access to digital media resources, film, audio, hosting, social media posts, reserved parking at chamber meetings, chambers of commerce certificate, discounts and exposure to new markets along with business networking opportunities.
