TARGET AUDIENCE

The target audience is male and female, ages 22yrs to 65yrs. Income ranges between $19,000 - $30,000/year.
Households consist mainly of married couples with kids, Single parents with kids, Single adults and Single elderly 65 years old.


SCENARIOS

What events are happening this month? • Best trails to run in town? • Good places to eat?
When do youth sports start? • Are there free activities for kids in town?
