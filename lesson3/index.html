SITE PLAN DESCRIPTION

The main purpose of www.myidahome.com website will be to serve the people of East Idaho in the towns of Preston, Soda Springs and Fish Haven. These towns are great places to live and mostly rural. Their populations range between 213 - 5,557 people.

The people are known to be down to earth, friendly and tight knit. Idaho expierence 4 seasons, so checking the weather conditios are important especailly to enjoy the many lakes they have for fishisng and the rurual areas to enjoy camping. These towns also have several activites and festivals they hold year round for the community.

PURPOSE

The purpose for the website myidahome.com is to provide the people of three towns in Eastern Idaho current weather conditions, town events and informational pages for each town.

The three towns are: Preston (Bear Lake County), Soda Springs (Caribou County) and Fish Haven (Bear Lake County).
