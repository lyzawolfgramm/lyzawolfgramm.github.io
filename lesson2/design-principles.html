Principles of Design | Lyza Wolfgramm

Contrast




Stance Socks

Stance socks has a great example of contrast. The colors pop out making it easy for the user to see the product. The white font stands out against the dark gray menu bar and on the bright yellow background of the socks product.

White Space and Clean Design




Apple

Apple always keeps it simple with the clean white background and the white space drawing your eyes to focus on the apple products their selling. The font is a good size and consistent with the nav bar in a grey making it easy to see the menu items.

Proximity




Zumiez

The images on this website along with the titles of products and items featured are all grouped together making it easier to know what category to choose and move through the website with ease.
